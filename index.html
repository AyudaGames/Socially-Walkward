HOW TO PLAY

YOU CAN'T STOP WALKING Once you put your headphones on, you keep moving forward. There's no pause, no backing up. The world keeps coming.
TAP TO TURN RIGHT Tap anywhere to turn 90° clockwise. That's it. ↻ You can ONLY turn right. No lefts. Ever. To go left, take three rights and accept your fate.
DODGE THE TALKERS Pink figures with speech bubbles are people you know. Walk past them and you'll be trapped in small talk forever.
STRANGERS ARE SAFE Grey figures are blissful strangers. They don't care about you. You don't care about them. Beautiful.
MEET FRIENDLIES Dogs, coffee carts, fellow headphone-wearers. Walk into the green glow for bonus points and a nicer time.
DAILY CHALLENGES A new goal every day. Complete them for streaks, achievements, and unlocks.
